Stomach, antrum, biopsy, Helicobacter gastritis.
[描述]
      The specimen submitted consists of two tissue fragments measuring up to 0.4x0.1x0.1 cm. in size fixed in formalin.

      Grossly, they are tan and elastic.

      All for section.   Jar 0

      Microscopically, the section shows Helicobacter gastritis, revealing mixed acute and chronic inflammation of lamina propria, accompanied by focal surface erosion, condensation of reticulin fibers in lamina propria, mild glandular atrophy and focal intestinal metaplasia. Some Helicobacter bacilli in the gastric pits are highlighted by immunostain. No malignancy is seen.

Stomach, body, polypectomy, fundic gland polyps.
[描述]
      The specimen submitted consists of eight tissue fragments measuring up to 0.3x0.3x0.3 cm. in size fixed in formalin.

      Grossly, they are tan and elastic.

      All for section.   Jar 0

      Microscopically, the section shows fundic gland polyps, composed by hyperplastic oxyntic glands with focal congestion. Some of these glands are cystically dilated. The overlying foveolae are short and blunt. No malignancy is seen.
